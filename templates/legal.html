Legal & Privacy Information

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Terms of Use

This website is provided as a service to the student body to facilitate the return of lost property. By using this site, you agree to submit accurate information regarding found items and honest claims regarding lost items. False reports or fraudulent claims may result in disciplinary action.

2. Privacy Policy

We take student privacy seriously.

  • Finder Information: The name and ID number of students reporting found items are stored securely in our database. This information is accessible only to authorized administrators and is never displayed publicly.
  • Claimant Information: Information submitted during a claim (Name, Contact Info) is used solely for the purpose of verifying ownership and coordinating the return of the item.
  • Cookies: This site uses a minimal session cookie to maintain administrator login states. No tracking cookies are used.

3. Item Retention Policy

Found items reported on this system will be held in the Main Office for a period of 60 days from the date of submission. Unclaimed items after this period may be donated to charity or disposed of, in accordance with school district policy.
